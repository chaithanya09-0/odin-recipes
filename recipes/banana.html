Banana Milkshake Recipe


Description

The Banana Milkshake is a creamy, naturally sweet drink that's full of energy. Itâ€™s made using ripe bananas, chilled milk, and a bit of sugar or honey. This shake is perfect for breakfast, post-workout refreshment, or as a quick, filling snack.

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1 cup cold milk
  • 1 tsp sugar or honey
  • A few ice cubes (optional)

Steps:

  1. Peel and slice the banana into small pieces.
  2. Add banana, milk, sugar/honey, and ice cubes to a blender.
  3. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  4. Pour into a glass and serve chilled.
