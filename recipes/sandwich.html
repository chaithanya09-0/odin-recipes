Veg Sandwich Recipe


Description

A Veg Sandwich is a quick, healthy, and light snack made with fresh vegetables like cucumber, tomato, and onion layered between slices of bread. It's perfect for breakfast, evening snacks, or even lunchboxes. You can customize it by adding cheese, lettuce, or chutneys for extra flavor.

Ingredients

  • 4 bread slices
  • 1 small cucumber (sliced)
  • 1 small tomato (sliced)
  • 1 small onion (sliced, optional)
  • Butter or mayonnaise
  • Salt & pepper

Steps:

  1. Spread butter or mayo on one side of each bread slice.
  2. Place sliced cucumber, tomato, and onion on two bread slices.
  3. Sprinkle a little salt and pepper.
  4. Cover with the remaining slices.
  5. Toast on a pan or sandwich maker (optional), or serve fresh.
