Maggie Recipe


Description

Masala Maggi is a spiced-up version of the classic instant noodles loved across India. It includes sautéed vegetables and extra spices, making it more flavorful and satisfying. It’s ready in under 10 minutes and is perfect for a quick meal or snack.

Ingredients

  • 1 pack Maggi noodles
  • 1 cup water
  • ½ onion (chopped)
  • ½ tomato (chopped)
  • ½ tsp red chilli powder
  • 1 tsp oil

Steps:

  1. Heat oil in a pan and sauté onions until soft.
  2. Add chopped tomatoes and cook for 2 minutes.
  3. Pour in water, add Maggi tastemaker and chilli powder.
  4. Add noodles and cook for 3-4 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Serve hot.
